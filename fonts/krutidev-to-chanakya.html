What is Krutidev Font?

Krutidev is one of the most widely used Hindi typing fonts in India. It is commonly used in government departments, typing exams, cyber cafes, printing presses, and desktop publishing work. Before Unicode became popular, Krutidev was the standard Hindi font for many Hindi typing applications.

Even today, many old Hindi documents, books, notices, and office files are still available in Krutidev format, which creates the need for reliable font conversion tools.

What is Chanakya Font?

Chanakya is another popular Hindi font mainly used in newspaper publishing, printing, magazines, and professional DTP work. It has been widely adopted by Hindi publishers because of its clean design and print-friendly appearance.

Since Krutidev and Chanakya use different character encoding systems, direct copy-paste does not work properly. This converter helps bridge that gap by accurately mapping characters between both fonts.
