Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is KrutiDev font and why is it used?

KrutiDev is a legacy Hindi font (Remington layout) widely used in Government Offices, Printing Press, and DTP Centers. It is still the standard for offline typing in many states.

Difference between KrutiDev and Unicode?
  • KrutiDev: Requires font installation to read. Specific to typing tests.
  • Unicode: Universal standard. Readable on all devices without installing fonts.
Is my data safe with EgConvert?
