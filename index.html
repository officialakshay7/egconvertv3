What is Unicode?

Unicode assigns a unique number to each character, regardless of platform, program, or language. The Unicode standard has been adopted by industry leading companies such as Apple, HP, IBM, Microsoft, Oracle, and many others.

Unicode is needed to support modern standards, such as XML, Java, JavaScript, and is ISO/IEC compliant. Incorporating Unicode into client-server or multi-dimensional devices and websites can result in significant cost savings over using traditional tools.
